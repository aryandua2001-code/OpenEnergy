About Us

Energy independence
at national scale.

OpenEnergy is an Indian EPC and BESS manufacturer dedicated to accelerating the country's transition to clean, dispatchable power. We design, build, and locally manufacture the storage that turns intermittent renewables into reliable infrastructure — so utilities, IPPs, and industries don't have to choose between affordability and reliability.

Founded on a single conviction: India will not import its way to a renewable grid. The cells, the engineering, the assembly, and the accountability all need to live in-country. OpenEnergy is built around that thesis.
